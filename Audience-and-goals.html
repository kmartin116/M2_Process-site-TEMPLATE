Conclusion and Final Thoughts

The primary overarching goals of the Slow Food Upstate website redesign is to boost membership, clarify the organization's mission and provide events/event information for the public.

Preliminary research of Slow Food Upstate - both primary and secondary - was much more helpful than I would have thought. This portion of the research was conducted before the interviews, so having a foundation of in-depth research about the organization really helped in defining goals and audiences.
